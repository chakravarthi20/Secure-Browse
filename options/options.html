Phishing Link Detection

Our extension helps detect phishing websites by analyzing links and alerting you when you visit potentially harmful pages, protecting you from scams and malicious attacks.

This feature helps users stay informed about potential risks while browsing, offering timely alerts and insights to protect their data and enhance browsing security.

Safe Download Feature $

Our extension ensures your downloads are secure by checking file URLs against reliable databases such as VirusTotal. If a file is flagged as potentially harmful, you will be notified before the download continues, giving you an added layer of protection against cyber threats.
