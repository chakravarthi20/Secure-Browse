Secure Browse Extension

Providing enhanced security through phishing detection, safe downloads, fake data injection, Dark Web Credential Check and VPN Tunneling for Individual Tabs.

Phishing Link Detection

Our extension helps detect phishing websites by analyzing links and alerting you when you visit potentially harmful pages, protecting you from scams and malicious attacks.

Fake Data Injection

To help you maintain your privacy, our extension can inject fake data into form fields on websites. This feature is useful for testing and security scenarios to safeguard your sensitive information.

Safe Download Feature

Our extension ensures your downloads are secure by checking file URLs against reliable databases such as VirusTotal. We scan files using multiple security engines (over 90+ engines) for threats including malware, phishing, and other malicious content. If a file is flagged as potentially harmful, you will be notified before the download continues, giving you an added layer of protection against cyber threats.

Dark Web Credential Check

Our extension offers the ability to check if your email and password have been exposed on the dark web using reliable data sources, providing timely warnings to protect your personal information.

Future Breach Notification

This feature monitors your registered email addresses for new breaches and alerts you as soon as your data appears in a new breach database. Stay informed and take action promptly to protect your personal data.

Domain Search

Allows verified domain owners to search for breached email addresses associated with their domain. Ideal for business users who want to monitor and secure their corporate accounts against data breaches.

VPN Tunneling for Individual Tabs

Enhance your browsing privacy by creating separate VPN tunnels for individual tabs in Chrome, ensuring data segregation and secure access per tab.
